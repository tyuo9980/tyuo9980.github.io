About Me

Hi, I'm Peter Li, an aspiring software engineer studying at the University of Waterloo. I was introduced to programming in grade 7 through games by trying to build third party tools and cheats for them. I love to play games competitively, and in high school I was able to finally design and develop my own from scratch. I enjoy developing software that people enjoy to use, whether it be solving a problem or letting them enjoy a few minutes of their time. I also enjoy to overcome challenges and learn about any new technology I encounter.

Aside from programming and games, I enjoy playing Piano, listening to Jazz, playing table tennis, and watching anime and those crazy Japanese variety shows. I'm also a hardcore competitive FPS gamer (I'm always up for a 1v1).
